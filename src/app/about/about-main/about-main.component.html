Humble Beginnings

A Colorado-Based startup company, founded by Jakob "Jake" Essig. 5280Free was founded after working at a local e-marketing company while finishing his Master's Degree in Software Engineering from Regis University. Jake saw the power an online presence with effective use of data collection and analytics had to transform the lives and businesses of his clients.

Jake has gone on to work as a Software Engineer at a Fortune 500 Engineering firm. While most of his work involves embedded systems and enterprise-level applications, web-based technologies hold a special place in his programming arsenal.
